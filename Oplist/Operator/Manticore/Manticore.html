Краткая Биография

Much of Manticore's history is unknown. It is known that prior to her employment at Rhodes Island, she had engaged in the "removal" of certain individuals. She has excellent stealth, infiltration, and surprise attack skills.
Now she serves as a special kind of Operator who provides these unique services for Rhodes Island.

Клинический анализ

Требуется уровень доверия: 25%

Imaging tests have shown the outlines of her internal organs to be indistinct due to abnormal shadows. Originium granules have been detected in her circulatory system, confirming her to be infected with Oripathy.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Manticore is gentle, shy, and sometimes even gives others the feeling that she's afraid. She speaks in a hesitant tone and often stammers. She rarely finishes a sentence in one attempt, and she appears very stiff when communicating with other Operators. She would probably be more comfortable talking to a rock.
Additionally, her rare invisibility ability has become a major hindrance to communication. It has completely prevented her from improving her interpersonal skills. It may be necessary to suggest additional treatment solutions to help Manticore develop her social skills.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


We communicate with Manticore by writing messages on paper. Oripathy has given her the ability to turn invisible (the report on this ability needs additional processing). It has been confirmed through interactive experiments that her ability does not transfer to inanimate objects, so paper communication has proved a suitable way to communicate with this operator.
After communication, Manticore likes to leave behind little gifts that smell sweetly of violets.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Manticore was born in Sargon. Because most of the background she provided could not be validated, Manticore's experience cannot be confirmed. What is known is that Manticore has undergone relatively comprehensive special operations training and has at least one year of operative experience under her belt.
The investigation into her special operations training unit was unsuccessful, and Sargon intelligence did not provide any valuable clues. Currently only unconfirmed and less than reliable intelligence from Sargon spies indicate there is a group of people who house Originium Arts users with special abilities in this area. These individuals are using special training methods to kill Sargon aristocrats or operatives from other nations.
Because of Manticore's vague background, she has been designated a special type of Oripathy patient at Rhodes Island. Any applications requesting use of Manticore in special operations or combat will first need to be submitted to Dr. Kal'tsit for approval.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


[Manticore's Ability's Test Record]
Test subject: Manticore.
Test objective: To probe the usefulness of the subject's abilities and Originium Arts.
Test content: To find Manticore while she is in hiding and to defend against her while she is in action.
Test participants: An Operator who has worked with Manticore on several missions.
Randomly select two Operators from all of the Operators available.
Among them must be at least one technician Operator or non-technician Operator with high Originium adaptability.
Test results: The test took place on a terrain simulating a complex environment. During the test period, none of the three Operators were able to locate the whereabouts of Manticore. When Manticore went on the offensive, some of the Operators managed to defend against her attacks using their quick reaction times and combat experience, while Manticore managed to successfully attack the others.
Feedback results: According to the explanations provided by the Operators, Manticore could not be captured using visual means. Some of the Operators with better Originium adaptability reported feeling a strange atmosphere that instantly made them as though they were in danger.
The above process has been recorded in its entirety. If you wish to view it, you must request it from the relevant department.
