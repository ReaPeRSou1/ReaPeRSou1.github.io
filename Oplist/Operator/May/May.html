Claims to be a Victorian Royal Detective; claims to have cracked a number of cases, and thus has gained some fame; claims to have been dispatched to Rhodes Island on high recommendations from her boss.
Regardless of what she claims to be, Ms. May is now serving Rhodes Island as a sniper.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


A self-proclaimed detective who appears to be rash and impetuous.
Even though she does exhibit some detective literacy, most likely having received professional training in investigation, tracking, counter-tracking, clue analysis, etc, she also emits an aura of unreliability, which can be a blessing in a sense.
Though her performance in minor, day-to-day matters leaves much to be desired, she always steps up in critical moments and demonstrates her trustworthiness as a detective.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Ms. May seems to be excessively concerned about Penguin Logistics, and often attempts to approach Exusiai and other staff members to ask questions about the internal operations of Penguin Logistics.
Ms. Croissant seems to welcome this development, and has even established a fee schedule for her information. It is unknown whether Ms. May actually pays this intelligence fee, but she claims to have received "valuable intel" from "enthusiastic Operators" such as Exusiai and others, including but not limited to the following:
1. Penguin Logistics is actually a food distribution company specializing in apple pie.
2. Penguin Logistics is actually an idol group that performs part-time courier services.
3. The boss of Penguin Logistics is actually a plushie toy.
4. Penguin Logistics rarely takes time off, but overtime pays very generously.
......

It's hard to say whether Ms. May believes this information.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Indeed, Ms. May's oral resume is fabricated.
According to subsequent investigations, Victoria does not have the position of "Royal Detective," and Ms. May herself is not a member of any official Victorian institution. Her real purpose in approaching Rhodes Island seems to be investigating Penguin Logistics.
Even though she acts as if her cover story is foolproof and when pressed upon the matter repeatedly, reiterates it as if buying into her own story, she compromised her own story even early in the interview process.
Due to her pitiably clumsy acting, one cannot help but to feel sorry for her. With Dr. Kal'tsit's consent, she was allowed to act freely under the condition that she would not do anything to undermine Rhodes Island's interests. After all, no matter how you look at it, she's far from discovering anything resembling the truth about Penguin Logistics.

Архивная запись 4

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Ms. May's love for the detective profession is true.
She is descended from a famous family of detectives. She received an upbringing that nurtured this ever since she was a child. In her room are a large number of cop movies and various detective props. If you entered her room without knowing better, you might be led to believe that you just barged into a tough guy's room.
Though Ms. May's performance still has much room for improvement, her dedication to the pursuit of truth deserves recognition.

Повышение

Разблокируется при повышении до Элиты II


Though she wouldn't admit it, Ms. May's joy upon receiving a promotion was almost palpable.
As long as she's been here, Ms. May has demonstrated confidence and optimism well beyond that of ordinary people, quickly adjusting her mentality in the face of whatever setback or failure she may have encountered. Outside evaluations also seem to have little effect upon her determination to pursue her goals. Nevertheless, in her mind, having her abilities be recognized by others may be the first step towards becoming a big detective.
