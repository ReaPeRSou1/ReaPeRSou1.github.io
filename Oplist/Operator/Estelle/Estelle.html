Краткая Биография

Estelle, her infection only at a moderate stage, attracted attention due to mutations triggered by Oripathy. She later sought medical asylum at Rhodes Island after trying several other treatment programs.
She received psychological counseling and a battery of examinations with her consent before officially joining Rhodes Island as a guard with a squad of operators.

Клинический анализ

Требуется уровень доверия: 25%

Imaging tests for this Operator showed blurry outlines of internal organs with visible dark spots. Unusually high concentrations of Originium particles were present in her circulatory system, indicating infection and confirming her as Infected at this stage.

[Поглощение клеток Ориджиниумом] 16%
Severe mutation following infection, sprouting horns noncharacteristic to the race. Close observation recommended.

[Плотность кристаллов Ориджиниума в крови] 0.31u/L
Infection entering mid-stage, tests required to discern specific effects.

[Mutation Assessment]
Based on current observations of Archosauria, double horns are not an expected racial trait. According to Operator Estelle's own account, she has never had any manifestation of biological mutations growing up. Thus, the mutated horns are determined to be caused by Oripathy.

Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Operator Estelle is habitually nervous and self-restrained, constantly in a state of unease, though most Operators have paid little attention to her state of mind. Estelle's two mutant horns are the primary cause of her worried behavior.
In the past, Estelle has slipped into depression over the mutation and withdrawn from all social activity. It is advised that the Doctor provide further psychological counseling to prevent total self-isolation.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Despite her typical baseline level of anxiety, Estelle has never appeared nervous in battle. Observations have shown that she retains her Archosaurian warrior spirit, as is present in Gavial, a fellow Archosaurian. Estelle possesses remarkable melee combat abilities, employing her own body as her primary weapon. Her enormous horns and powerful tail have won her many a victory, as she charges mightily ahead, driven by bestial instinct.
Her fellow operators have stood in awe of her ferocity. To which Estelle responds with uncharacteristic withdrawal, opting for solo combat and giving her teammates a wide berth, focusing only on her commander's orders.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 200%


Currently, Rhodes Island has established a series of special arrangements for Operator Estelle, including psychological evaluation and disease management. Due to Estelle’s expression of a strong desire to recover, it is best to avoid explaining the situation to her for the time being.
Based upon current medical technology, the prospects of containing Estelle’s Oripathy are more realistic. Monitoring of the patient’s psychological condition has shown that she is more willing to “accept the disease as a part of her and live in a non-human form,” which is a positive development. However, it is possible that this will be a source of instability in the future.
Based upon this assessment, the relevant personnel will must continue to evaluate and adjust their methods in order to actively guide Estelle to proper self-acknowledgement.
