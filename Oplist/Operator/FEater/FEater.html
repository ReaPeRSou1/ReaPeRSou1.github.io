Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


FEater studied martial arts for many years prior to entering the film industry. She is the heir to the Tie Yi Liu He Quan, the Universal Fist of the Iron Will, one of Yen's great martial arts traditions. FEater has mastered all Five Elements, Eight Strikes, and Twelve Forms of her art, giving her the ability to deal devastating blows in the blink of an eye.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


After contracting Oripathy, FEater's acting career and aspirations came to a screeching halt. But she didn't give into depression like one might expect, since movies to her were just one vehicle for delivering righteousness. The optimistic former star quickly landed back on her feet.
