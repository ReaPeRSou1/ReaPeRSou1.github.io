Архивные записи

Архивная запись 1

Требуется уровень доверия: 50%


Greyy was born in a city in Bolívar. His father was an unskilled worker and his mother worked in a care home. Both of them were in poor health. With both parents earning very little, the family barely made ends meet.
Greyy's childhood was much different from that of his peers. He is introverted and practical like his father, and he is physically weak like his mother. However, Greyy has a knack for Arts. He would have had a promising career on the back of his talent, would that he didn't get infected.

Архивная запись 2

Требуется уровень доверия: 100%


Greyy is decently skilled in the use of electricity and Originium. By quickly increasing the voltage of an operating unit, he can make the positive and negative ions collide and create energy.
He learned about Originium from his mother, though he is unsure why an ordinary person like her would have had such a detailed knowledge of Arts.
He lived in a slum that suffered from regular blackouts. Because of this, Greyy decided to collect discarded Originium fragments near a smelting plant. He focused their energy into his staff to create a temporary light source.
The staff, of course, was also made using repurposed materials.

Архивная запись 3

Требуется уровень доверия: 150%


Greyy lost his family, so joining Rhodes Island has an extra special meaning for him. His mastery of electrical energy Arts got him a job maintaining the generators at Rhodes Island.
Greyy is honest, quiet, deferential and always grateful that he gets to be of help to Rhodes Island.
Perros are known for their loyalty. Even unlucky Perros who are infected, like Greyy, will cherish any companions who share the same difficult and lonely path.
